Feel fabulous wrapped in this sumptuous 48" coat with a standup collar that converts to a shawl collar. Experience the dramatic elegance of a snowy White Mink coat that oozes of luxury and glamour. Pelt lines emphasize the lush, plush feel of the World's finest faux fur, soft in texture and light in weight. Perfect for everything from black tie events to romantic dinners, expect to feel like a snow princess wrapped in cozy warmth! From seriously soft velvet pockets and a sumptuous satin lining to European fur hooks and a satin-faced hem, we craft into this coat every detail you'd expect in a $30,000 coat.
